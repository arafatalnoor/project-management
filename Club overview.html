Official Instagram Account

"Instagram Rasmi Kelab Teknologi Maklumat, Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology, Tun Hussein Onn University Malaysia" refers to the official Instagram account of the Information Technology Club (Kelab Teknologi Maklumat) associated with the Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology at Tun Hussein Onn University Malaysia (UTHM).

Platform Purpose

This account serves as a platform to:

  1. Share Club Activities: Updates on events, workshops, competitions, and projects conducted by the club.
  2. Connect with Students: Engage with students within the faculty or the university, as well as with external audiences interested in IT-related activities.
  3. Promote Opportunities: Announce collaboration opportunities, internships, or networking events for students and members.
  4. Celebrate Achievements: Highlight the accomplishments of the club and its members, such as competition wins or community outreach initiatives.
